by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Jngres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviè was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvte. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviè family in the artist painted that year.
